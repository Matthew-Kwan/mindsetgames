Mindset Games seek to expand the capacity of the brain through mini games that are associated with pleasure, creativity, and interaction.

Strengthen and exercise the brain

Mindset Games was produced with the idea that by implementing a simple object that is both sensory-stimulating and multifunctional, this object could be used as a tool to encourage older adults to make use of physical and cognitive abilities to strengthen and exercise the brain.

Mindset games have focuses on four domains

Following "Play Intervention for Dementia" (PID) and the framework of "Strategies and Skills Learning and Development" (SSLD), individuals can explore, re-explore, or re-visit forgotten or ignored abilities.

Benefits and Results:

Participants were observed to have:

  • positive shift in mood
  • motivation
  • engagement
  • improved self-expression
  • improved interpersonal communication
  • less disruptive behaviour
  • enhanced cognitive abilities such as memory and language

"The care-givers' feedbacks have been positive and found their loved ones "revived". I noticed various changes not only from our service users at Yee Hong but also in care-givers, volunteers as well as our frontline staff."
